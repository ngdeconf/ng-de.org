For the community, by the community

NG-DE is the first community-driven Angular conference in Germany. It will take place in Berlin, 2025. A bunch of one-day workshops are planned which can be booked separately from the main event.

A conference is more than mate-tea and wifi

We create an open, diverse and welcoming conference with fantastic speakers and a warm and friendly environment. Barista coffee and childcare are just a few of the benefits we want to offer.

If you are interested to become a sponsor, we appreciate if you get in touch with us via sponsoring@ng-de.org
